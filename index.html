Focus Areas

The workstreams I am focused on through scenario analysis, writing practice, and AML case review.

Transaction Monitoring

Sanctions Screening

Assessing name-match relevance, contextual identifiers, and the difference between apparent similarity and meaningful match risk.

KYC / CDD Alignment

Comparing expected customer behavior to observed activity so inconsistencies and documentation gaps become visible early.

Investigative Documentation

Writing summaries that explain what was reviewed, what matters, what remains unclear, and why escalation is or is not justified.

Case Studies

Short examples of how I would structure review, test assumptions, and document reasoning in common AML scenarios.

Structuring Review

Repeated Cash Deposits Below Reporting Thresholds

A scenario built around repeated sub-threshold deposits over a compressed time window.

  • Assess whether the activity pattern appears intentionally segmented rather than naturally occurring.
  • Compare volume, frequency, and branch usage against the expected customer profile and account purpose.
  • Document what supports escalation and what additional facts would reduce uncertainty.
Write-up format ready for publication or recruiter review.
Sanctions Alert Review

Potential Name Match With Limited Identifiers

A sanctions-screening scenario focused on separating weak similarity from actionable match risk.

  • Test name similarity against available identifiers such as geography, DOB, and customer context.
  • Separate evidence that reduces risk from evidence that is still missing or inconclusive.
  • Write a decision trail that another reviewer can follow without guessing at the logic.
Built to demonstrate reasoning quality, not just outcome.
